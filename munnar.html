Munnar – The Enchanting Hill Station🌄

"Munnar – where the mountains wear a blanket of tea gardens, the clouds whisper to the hills, and nature paints its masterpiece in every shade of green ." 🌄🍃

Munnar, a scenic hill station in Kerala, is renowned for its sprawling tea plantations, misty hills, and cool climate. Top attractions include Eravikulam National Park, Mattupetty Dam, and Top Station. Visitors can enjoy trekking, boating, and wildlife spotting. The local cuisine and picturesque waterfalls add to Munnar's charm, making it a perfect getaway for nature lovers.

Must visit in Munnar

  • Eravikulam National Park
  • Top Station
  • Mattupetty Dam & Lake
  • Tea Gardens & Tea Museum
  • Attukal Waterfalls
