Kashmir - "If there is a paradise on earth, it is this, it is this, it is this."

Kashmir, nestled in the northernmost region of India, is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, snow-capped mountains, serene lakes, and lush green valleys. Known as "Paradise on Earth," it offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, adventure, and tranquility. The region's rich culture, warm hospitality, and vibrant handicrafts make it a dream destination for travelers.

  • Gulmarg
