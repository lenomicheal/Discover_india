Ooty – The Queen of Hill Stations

"Ooty – where the clouds kiss the hills and time slows down in nature's embrace."

Ooty (Udhagamandalam), nestled in the Nilgiri Hills, is a breathtaking hill station renowned for its cool climate, lush tea gardens, and picturesque landscapes. Known for its colonial charm, Ooty offers scenic beauty, serene lakes, and vibrant botanical gardens, making it a perfect getaway for nature lovers and honeymooners.

Must visit in Ooty

  • Ooty Lake
  • Doddabetta Peak
  • Government Botanical Garden
  • Nilgiri Mountain Railway
